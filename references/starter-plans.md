# Default Starter Program (3-Day Toned & Strong)

## Day 1 - Upper + Core
Warm-up
- 5 min incline walk
- shoulder mobility + light activation

1. Lat pulldown or assisted pull-up - 3 x 8-10
2. Seated row - 3 x 10
3. Chest press machine - 3 x 10
4. Shoulder press machine - 3 x 10
5. Lateral raise - 3 x 12
6. Rope triceps pushdown - 3 x 12
7. Dumbbell or cable curl - 3 x 10
8. Crunch machine or dead bug - 3 x 12

Substitutions
- Chest press busy -> DB bench press
- Seated row busy -> 1-arm DB row
- Shoulder press busy -> DB shoulder press

## Day 2 - Lower + Glutes
Warm-up
- 5 min treadmill walk
- bodyweight squat + glute activation

1. Leg press - 3 x 10-12
2. Romanian deadlift - 3 x 8-10
3. Hip thrust / glute drive - 3 x 10
4. Hamstring curl - 3 x 10
5. Hip abductor - 3 x 12
6. Hip adductor - 3 x 12
7. Calf raise - 3 x 12
8. Optional plank/crunch - 2-3 rounds

Substitutions
- Leg press busy -> goblet squat to box
- Glute drive busy -> DB hip thrust
- Hamstring curl busy -> cable pull-through or extra RDL set

## Day 3 - Full Body + Conditioning
Warm-up
- 5-8 min treadmill
- dynamic mobility

1. DB RDL or hinge machine - 3 x 10
2. Goblet squat or leg press - 3 x 10
3. Lat pulldown - 3 x 10
4. Chest press or incline DB press - 3 x 10
5. Cable face pull - 3 x 12
6. Walking lunge / split squat - 2-3 x 8 each side
7. Core circuit - 2-3 rounds
8. Incline walk - 10-20 min

Substitutions
- Lunge discomfort -> step-up, supported split squat, or leg extension
